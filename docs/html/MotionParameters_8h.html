Computes the instantaneous heaving velocity vector.

Calculates the heaving velocity of an airfoil at a given time, based on the derivative of the sinusoidal heaving motion. The velocity is returned as a 3D vector, with the y-component representing the heaving velocity (negative sign indicates downward velocity is positive).

Parameters
h1Heaving amplitude (meters).
phi_hHeaving phase angle (radians).
tCurrent time (seconds).
omegaAngular frequency (radians/second).
Returns
VectorXd A 3D vector [0, -h_dot, 0] where h_dot is the heaving velocity.
