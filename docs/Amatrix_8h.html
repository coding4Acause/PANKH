Computes the A matrix for the vortex panel method.

This function calculates the entire coefficient matrix with the help of panel coefficient or panel influence matrix based on the control points and panel points of the airfoil. It also writes the computed matrix to a file.

Parameters
n# panel vertices nodes on airfoil geometry
AReference to the matrix where computed values will be stored.
x_cpVector of x-coordinates of control points.
y_cpVector of y-coordinates of control points.
x_ppVector of x-coordinates of panel points.
y_ppVector of y-coordinates of panel points.
